Have any questions? Check below to see if it's already answered! Otherwise, feel free to fill out the form and we will get back to you as soon as possible!

Where and when does the club meet? ▼

The club meets on Wednesday after school in D103, typically for 1 hour.

Do I need my own computer? ▼

You do not need to bring your own computer although it is recommended as pc availability can become an issue. Additionally, if you are placed in the advanced group, it is highly encouraged to bring your own computer as it may be needed for various software.

Do I already need to know how to code? ▼

No! The club welcomes people of all skill levels. Both the person with no experience and years of experience will be challenged, encouraged, and learn in this club.

How do I become an officer? ▼

Officer applications happen at the end of every school year and the new officers are announced shortly after.
